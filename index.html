About HealthPlus Asia

HealthPlus Asia is a unique commemoration of Health, Wellness and Fitness service providers, consultants, institutions and products/ under one roof.

From infant care, nutrition for kids, protection against pollution and life style disorders, combating high stress levels and insomnia, cancer prevention and cure, and elderly care, diagnostic services and doctor visits at the door step to innovation like tele-medicine, organ transplants, dental reconstructions, hair transplants and bariatric treatments, HealthPlus Asia gives you exclusive access to world class health care under one roof.

At HealthPlus Asia you get to indulge in mainstream medical sciences like Allopathy and also popular alternative medicines like Homeopathy, Aayurveda. You gain premium access to Indias best Gyms, Yoga retreats, Rehabilitation centers and Spas. Our other exhibits like nutritional supplements, organic product and health insurance are your gateway for a healthy living.
